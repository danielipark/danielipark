Hello

My name is Daniel Park and I am a UX/UI Designer.


If you're reading this I probably already like you. I've always been so interested in people's stories, passions and interests. Hearing about the time you laughed so hard that you almost peed yourself or when you finally got your dream job, makes me genuinely happy.


I am an active and empathetic listener and it has helped me immensely in all of my personal projects. Although prioritizing the user in user-centered solutions seems like common sense, it is often overlooked. Getting caught up in standard processes or beautiful designs is easy to do, however positive user feedback and results drive my purpose.


I would love to hear all your stories and more importantly problems. As a UX/UI Designer, I thrive on creating solutions, so let's get this conversation started.
